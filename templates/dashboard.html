Tips & Advice for Using StockSmart
  • Getting Started: Add your products first (Products → Add Product). Include cost price and selling price.
  • Recording Sales: Use the "Sell" page to quickly record a sale. Stock updates automatically.
  • Low Stock: Check "Low Stock" page regularly. Set thresholds for each product.
  • Reports: Use filters to see sales by day, week, month. Export CSV for accounting.
  • Tax Reminders: Enter your TIN and VAT registration status in your Profile to get reminders.
  • Email Alerts: Make sure your email is correct in Profile to receive low stock and tax alerts.
  • Liquid Products: Use units like liter, ml, or gallon. Enter decimal quantities when selling.
  • AI Forecast: See predicted sales for next 7 days based on your history.
  • Anomaly Detection: AI alerts you when sales are unusually high or low.
